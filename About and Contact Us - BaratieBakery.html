Welcome To Baratie Bakery

Baratie Bakery first opened its doors in 2020 on 007, Konoha, Hidden Leaf, Skypiea.

It was one of the few bakeries, if not the very first, to offer delivery service in the city!

In 2021, the Baratie Bakery unveiled its special products that instantly became an internet sensation, attracting customers from around the country to attempt to eat the products.

What many people hadn't expected was to find amazing products that are more than just a bakery item.
